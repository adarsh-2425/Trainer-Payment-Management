ICT TRAINER PAYMENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM


ICT Pay, the Trainer Payment Management System of ICT is designed to carry out all the trainer payments in a hassle free manner and on time. It consists of 3 modules-Admin, Trainer and Finance Team member. The trainer have to add all the training details of a particular month in the timesheet provided. Then admin will approve this timesheet and calculate the payment. The generated timesheet is sent to each trainers dashboard. The finance team can view the payment timesheets in pdf format in their dashboard. The complete system has a simple setup and user friendly design, which enables all the users to carry out their functionalities in a convenient way.
